⇣ Internet as Body

Internet as Body is an experimental website that explores the ways our physical bodies extend to the web. This is explored through the feminist glitch and user/artist interactions.

⇣ Yassified Billy Bass

Yassified Billy Bass is part of an ongoing project that examines the "yassifying" of household kitsch items. Traditionally a masculine object this piece is presented in a more feminine and queer way. It calls into questioning our gender expectations on objects as well as the many recent redesigns of typical consumerist items. It was displayed at The Funny Stuff Show in Redhook, NY.

⇣ Ilovguys5675

Ilovguys5675 is a short film that explores my experience with camming through a digital, campy, and comedic lens. The purpose was to research and explore ways that computer interfaces can be used for narratives and character building.
